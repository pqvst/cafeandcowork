Very work friendly chain cafe operated by Dotour. Interior is very nice, lots of single counter seats with outlets. Ambient music is quite loud but it is a piano song so not too disturbing. WiFi is the same as any other dotour cafes, you'd have to re-connect every hour. It would be better if the popup was as automatic as the Tully's one.e. But that's more on KDDI who provides Wi2. Props to USEN for better UX.
