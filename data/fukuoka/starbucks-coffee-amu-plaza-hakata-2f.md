A starbucks that is directely connected to the Hakata Station. Great for getting some stuff done around the station but not a place to stay for long. The seats with low chairs and sofas are the tiniest tables in the Starbucks history. Also the stool seats are fine but the stools are the most uncomfortable ones that you might as well just stand. WiFi is fine.
