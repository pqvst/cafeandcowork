A craft coffee roaster located in the basement floor of a department store. The coffee is excellent but the place is quite small and there are no power outlets. The café doesn't have its own WiFi so you have to use the department store's shared guest WiFi which is pretty slow by Korean standards. The place doesn't seem to get busy at any time or day so it's always a good option. It can be a bit tricky to find as it's located next to the makeup area and not near the other restaurants in the store.
